Welcome to the Swiss Dairy Explorer

Over the past 25 years, the Swiss dairy industry has undergone profound structural and economic transformations. The Swiss Dairy Explorer is an interactive journey through a quarter-century of agricultural data, visualizing the delicate balance between milk production, consumption, and the financial pressures facing local farmers. By correlating diverse government datasets, this project goes beyond static numbers to reveal the hidden relationships shaping the landscape of Swiss agriculture today.

Cattle headcount by canton

How many cattle each Swiss canton reports, year by year. The numbers are the official Identitas AG series on opendata.swiss — Rinder: Entwicklung nach Kantonen; we add up the monthly rows so you get one total per canton per calendar year. The map and the ranking on the right always match the year on the slider. Click a canton or a bar to highlight the same place in both views; click the grey map background to clear.

Raw milk uses and dairy production

Where exactly does all that milk go once it leaves the farm? The journey from pasture to plate is complex. The Sankey diagram below traces raw milk as it is divided, processed, and allocated across a diverse array of finished dairy products. By visualizing these streams, we can see exactly which goods dominate the Swiss dairy market and how raw resources are utilized.
